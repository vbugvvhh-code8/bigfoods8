# BigFoods Vision

## Mission
Build Africa's most trusted and intelligent food delivery ecosystem.

## Core Goals
- Delight customers with fast, reliable ordering.
- Help restaurants grow through technology.
- Empower riders with fair, transparent earnings.
- Build a scalable, secure platform.
- Keep operating costs efficient while maintaining quality.

## Product Philosophy
Every feature should solve a real operational problem, scale cleanly, and provide an excellent user experience across all screen sizes.
